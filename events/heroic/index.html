  • Basics
    Heroic is an event that is perfect for new players to get stronger. In heroic, your goal is to complete harder versions of the different stages to get materials that go towards a pretty strong armor from last month. The stages are split into 3 types:
    • Novice
    • Intermediate
      • Novice and intermediate both have the same formula. Each stage needs to be beaten 8 times to get a star crystal, and there are 6 star crystals used to craft the heroic armor. There are 6 stages in each novice and intermediate. Therefore, you can beat the novice stages first, craft the heroic armor, then work on the intermediate stages in the time left so that you can make the + version of the heroic armor.
    • Hardcore
      • Hardcore has slightly harder stages, but each stage only needs to be beaten 1 time. Once you beat a hardcore stage, you get heroic scrolls, which can be exchanged in the shop for an exclusive material, as well as the set for the heroic armor.
  • Heroic Cheese

    Heroic cheese is a strategy that is used to make heroic mode considerably easier, and can be used by new players to clear almost the entirety of heroic mode without having any armor requirements. The only requirement is that you have 10 or more strong friends (preferably with the most recent UA) .

    To do heroic cheese, the steps are:

    • Enter the stage with 1 knight and 1 friend,
    • Go through the stage until the friend is almost dead,
    • Force close the game,
    • Re-enter the game,
    • Repeat until the stage is complete.

  • Strategy

    Once you have 6 star crystals, you should immediately start crafting the heroic armor. It will take a few days to complete, and you need to have it crafted, so you can level it up slightly to craft the + version.

    Once you have fully collect all 12 star crystals, wait until your heroic armor is done crafting, level it to roughly level 40, until you get a notification telling you that the + version is craftable, then craft that.

    Regarding heroic scrolls, it depends on what your goals are. If you are a new player trying to get stronger, but not going after the exclusive, you should spend the scrolls first on the set for the heroic armor, then silver coins are likely a good next choice. For later game players, priority is always to get the exclusive material, then it's up to preference whether you get the coins, set, or other items.
