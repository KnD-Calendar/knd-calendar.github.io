  • Basics
    Pets are a very important part of the game, and having strong pets is critical for your success in events. Here are pet abilities you should watch for, in order of leveling priority.
    • Shatter Shield
    • Special Regeneration (Primal only)
    • Durable Rock Shield (Primal only)
    When using pets, you want them to have the element that matches with as many of the armors your team is wearing as possible, as knights get a small boost from the pets when their elements match.
  • Shatter Shield
    Shatter Shield pets held the top spot for strongest pets for a long time, and are still by far the strongest for weaker game content, although now they are outdone by Special Regeneration pets for high level raid bosses. When you acquire one of these pets, try to evolve it to tier 4, then you will unlock the Shatter Shield ability. Keep an eye out for any of these as a new player, as they will be a huge power boost.
    Shatter Shield Pets
  • Special Regeneration
    Special Regeneration pets are better than Shatter Shield pets at late game content, such as level 5+ raid bosses. When looking for Special Regeneration pets, they must meet a few requirements:
    • They must be Primal (a P follows their information, such as [S80P])
    • The Special Regeneration ability must be the last ability on the list
    Example Primal Special Regeneration Pet
  • Durable Rock Shield
    Durable Rock Shield is no longer as good as Special Regeneration, but can still be useful, especially in arena. Durable Rock Shield pets also must be primal, but that is the only requirement. These should be done only in the very late game, if at all.
