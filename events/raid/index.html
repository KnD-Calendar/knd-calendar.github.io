  • Basics
    Raid is an event where High Commanders and above scout bosses for everyone in the guild to attack for points. The idea is simple, group up, and hit it until it dies!
  • Blitz Wars
    Blitz raid is a type of raid event that is much quicker, and always comes the day after a war ends.
  • Time (Raid)
    Raid events run every other Friday from 19:00 UTC () to Monday 19:00 UTC (), exactly 3 days.
    Time (Blitz Raid)
    Blitz Raid events run the day after a War event ends, Tuesday from 19:00 UTC () to Wednesday 22:00 UTC (), 1 day and 3 hours.
  • Time (Raid)

    For a new player, your best plan of attack is to go after level 2s until you can defeat them in 1 attack, then try 3s to see if you get more points on 3s. Once you get more points on 3s than 2s, keep attacking them.

    When attacking, it is usually better to have an armor with no weakness and no strength against the raid elements, rather than an armor with a strength and a weakness against the raid elements, unless there is a large gap in stats.

    For pets, you will want to use Shatter Shield pets for low level bosses (2s/3s), and anything higher you will want Special Regeneration. See the pets page for more information on pets.

  • Milestones
    As you progress through the game and start chasing the UA, you will be collecting exclusive materials from raids. The milestones where you get exclusive materials are:
    • 45k points
    • 200k points
